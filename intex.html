We only use the highest quality ingredients

The Slab is based on a simple, nostalgic idea: paying tribute to the original American ice cream parlor – built on quality ice cream, delicious
toppings and a fun and inviting experience.

Supporting our local farmers

Ice cream with the look and taste of an old favorite. Every bite melts in your mouth to leave a creamy, rich aftertaste. All of this is a direct
result of our support for local farms with the best ingredients. Supplied fresh every morning, for every batch.

Special flavors added every week

If your flavor is selected, you receive 10 free cones on us!
